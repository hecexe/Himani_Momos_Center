About Us

Crafting the bold street-food experience one steamy plate at a time.

Himani MOMOS Center brings the buzz of the street corner into a premium dining vibe. We prepare handmade momos, layer every filling with fresh ingredients, and finish each order with signature spicy chutneys and smoky sauces.

From classic steamed bites to crunchy fried favorites and blazing tandoori platters, our kitchen is built for flavor lovers who want comfort, speed, and unforgettable spice.
